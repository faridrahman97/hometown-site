Visit San Jose

Enjoy tech culture and city life in this beautiful town in the heart of Silicon Valley. Home to several tech companies!

Top three activities to do at San Jose

Go for a nice walk in a calming area

The Japanese Friendship Garden is an amazing spot that is great for a walk with family and friends.

Explore, eat, and enjoy live music

Watch a live band perform at Santana Row while you eat, shop, and enjoy at the different stores and restaurants.

Watch a live event

The SAP Center is home to the San Jose Sharks. You can catch a game and root for the hometown team!
